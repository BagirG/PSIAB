Public Education

CISRC offers public education that benefits the progress and development of Muslim-Christian and interdisciplinary relations. It believes that deep and careful thinking can influence issues in public life. Therefore, the institute will facilitate education and discussion on topics oriented towards current issues. In addition, learning programs are also designed for religious practitioners who wish to take time out to think deeply about specific issue(s). Such education will take the form of public lectures, seminars, webinars, symposia, and learning modules on a national and international scale.

Scriptural Studies

One of the leading programs is the monthly Muslim-Christian scripture study group, where participants from Muslim and Christian backgrounds come together to explore how individuals from different faiths understand their sacred texts and the sacred texts of others. The study group, known as "Scriptural Reasoning," discusses short passages from both scriptures, particularly those related to universal themes. It considers how Muslim and Christian communities can commit and collaborate in applying the insights gained to everyday life. These activities always begin with fellowship and refreshments.
